# Path of Totality
In astronomy, "totality" refers to the state or period of an eclipse when light from
the eclipsed body is totally obscured. 

Totality is a MUD/MOO/M* roleplaying game of transhuman science-fiction with pervasive survival and cosmic horror elements. Set 10 years after the Fall of Earth, 
when devastating godlike TITAN AIs destroyed most of civilization, what remains of 
transhumanity and her children are scattered across the solar system. 

From floating aerostats on Venus, to habitat domes on Luna, to the hinterlands of a partially terraformed Mars, to the moons of Jupiter and Saturn, and beyond, transhumanity struggles to survive in the wake of the apocalypse. All the while, everyone looks to the stars and wonders if the TITANs will ever return to finish the job they started.

Based on the Eclipse Phase roleplaying game.

# Eclipse Phase 
Your mind is software. Program it.
Your body is a shell. Change it.
Death is a disease. Cure it.
Extinction is approaching. Fight it.

An “eclipse phase” is the period of time between when a cell is infected by a virus and when the virus appears within the cell and transforms it. During this period, the cell does not appear to be infected, but it is. 

Eclipse Phase is published by Posthuman Studios, LLC. Additional information on the

# Planned Features
Fingers crossed, haha.
- Three initial worlds:
   - Mars. Partially terraformed but the most populous planet in transhuman space.
   - Jupiter. Largest planet in the solar system with a massive system of moons.
   - Titan. The largest moon of Saturn. A cold and dark mirror of Earth.
- Three initial factions:
   - The Planetary Consortium. A conglomeration of hypercorporations that promote capitalist and market ideologies.
   - The Jovian Republic. A bioconservative, militaristic polity with the largest space fleet and weapon reserves.
   - The Autonomist Alliance. A loose collection of anarchists, technosocialists, libertarians, political extremists, and other brinker types that have banded together for mutual defense.
- Percentile skill+stat based system, roll under but high, blackjack rules.
- A realtime turnbased combat system simulation.
- A realized space based world wide web called the Mesh, with both AR and VR implementations.
- Augmented reality commands for in-character communications: email, DMs, vidchat, etc.
- Virtual reality: simulspaces, experiential playbacks, altlives, etc.
- Separation of character egos from character morphs.
- Backup insurance and farcasting.
- Ego forking.
